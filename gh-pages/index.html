Bierfit

A beer fitness tracker.

Build your own MeiDeckel

MeiDeckel is the base device you need to track the sips taken, the level of beer in your bottle and send this data to bierfit application servers that will further process, analyze and present your information.

Connect your MeiDeckel

Within your Bierfit profile settings you can register a new device. Use the given Api-Key to setup a connection. Your registered account will be the default user, if you are using a keypad and your friends joined your device, you can switch the user at any time.

Use your MeiDeckel to remember... hmm... beer

As soon as you successfully setup the MeiDeckel and the device is online, a signal batch in your users profile will show you that Beerfit is currently tracking your consumption data.

This project is under heavy development and details may change soon. If you want to be an early tester please contact us before setting up a MeiDeckel and propably dealing early issues. We are looking forward to hear from you as well as join you for a beer.
